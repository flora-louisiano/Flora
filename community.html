Community

Baguio City is seen from the vicinity of the province of Benguet and is a part of the Cordillera Administrative Region. It is located in the region of Luzon, Philippines. The population of the city according to the 2010 census, jumps to a number of 318,676 individuals in the city.

The city is labeled as The Summer Capital of the Philippines, because of the city's altitude. The inhabitants of the city vary from distinct cultures and such languages include the following: Ilocano, Ibaloi,Tagalog, Kankana-ey,Kapampangan,Pangasinan,Chinese and English. Baguio city is currently led by Mauricio Domogan;mayor,Edison Bilog;vice mayor, and Nicasio Aliping Jr.;congressman.

Saint Louis University is one of the most acclaimed and respected institutions. It has surpassed so many years of sharing exemplary education to the people. It has been a part of a major role in Baguio city's growth which provided quality education to the mass. The sustainable development of the school has led tons of success in becoming the center of education in the North.

The population of Saint Louis University reaches to thousands of students about 30,000 and more, which consists of the elementary, high school and the college department. The vision-mission of the SLU serves as the pathfinder of the schools virtue for their students, for accomplishing progress and success. It embodies the value of social involvement, creativity, competence and Christian spirit.

Saint Louis University is cited as the Light of the North, for providing selfless services not only to the city, but to the people. SLU gave light to the city and the people passed it on for greater endeavors and better change.
