Technology

We used the following equipment and programs:

  • A) Laptop - We used this for making the documents, editing photos, saving files and also making the webpage.
  • A1) Microsoft Word 2013 - This was used for creating the articles/documents.
  • A2) Adobe Photoshop Creative Suite 6 - We used this for editing the images, and making the website wallpaper and banner.
  • A3) Sony Vegas Pro 13 - This was used for editing the videos, including the interviews and others.
  • A4) Notepad ++ - This was used for constructing the webpage.
  • A5) Google Chrome - This was used for browsing, and uploading files.
  • A6) Mozilla Firefox - We used this as our host and site tester.
  • B) DSLR (digital single-lens reflex camera) - establish a well-formed gallery of pictures, as well as high resolution videos of the interviews.
  • C) Mobile Wifi - We used this as a portable source for the internet.
  • D) Voice Recorder - We used this for recording the interviews.
  • E) Mobile Phone - This is our primary means of communication.
