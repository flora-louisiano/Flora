Relevance

Engage. Our project talks about the Panagbenga Flower Festival. As citizens of Baguio and as responsible students, we are enticed to continue our living culture and tradition not only to influence and share, but also to pass it on the beauty of Panagbenga to the others as well. It is possible because of this project, we engage ourselves in opening our eyes and revealing what the people does not know about the festival.

Unite. 40 students of different preferences are bestowed with talents which are very distinct. This project will not be made without the exerted efforts of every individual. We may have some struggles, although, as socially involved and competent students, we pushed through beyond our capabilities.Even though time was upon us, we managed to finish this entry, because of one reason, we were united.
